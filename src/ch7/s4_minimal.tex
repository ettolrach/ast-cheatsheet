\wde{n.n.} We define $\Z^* = \Z \setminus \{ 0 \}$. For section 7.4, $\sim$ is the relation on $\Z \times \Z^*$ defined by $(a, b) \sim (a', b') \iff ab' = a'b$ ($a, a' \in \Z, b, b' \in \Z^*$). (Lemma 7.4.1) $\sim$ is an equiv. rel.
\wde{7.4.3 $\Q$} $\Q$ is $(\Z \times \Z^*) / \sim$.
\wpr{7.4.9} $\Q$ is a field, (Lemma 7.4.12) $\leq_{\Q}$ a total order, (lemma 7.4.13) $\Q$ an ordered field.
